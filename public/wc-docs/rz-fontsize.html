rz-fontsize

Increase or decrease the fontsize.


  • S: smaller than defined/default font-size (~ -2px)
  • M: Medium - same as defined/default font-size
  • L: larger than defined/default font-size (~ +2px)
  • XL: x-larger than defined/default font-size (~ +4px)

Syntax

Within HTML header: 
<script type="module" src="/assets/rz-fontsize.js"></script>
Within HTML body: 
<rz-fontsize> <rz-fontsize/>
